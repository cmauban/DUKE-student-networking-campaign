Networking Do's & Don'ts

    • Tip #1

      Don't send a generic email blast to a ton of alumni all at once.

      Do learn about alumni before you contact them.

    • Tip #2

      Don't be vague about your intentions.

      Do ask for advice and guidance.

    • Tip #3

      Don't spam alumni with repeated requests.

      Do follow up politely if you don't hear back in a few business days.
